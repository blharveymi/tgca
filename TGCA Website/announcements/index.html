2016 Upcoming Events
posted by Web Master on Sep 11, 2016
1.  September 27 (Tuesday) TGCA Annual Meeting 7 p.m. at the Saratoga Elementary School Cafeteria - Please join us for our Annual Meeting. The Board will be presenting plans for the Tennis Court and Basketball Court at the Soccer Field. The association will be voting on a special assessment to cover the cost of the plans.
 
2.  September 19 (Monday) Board Meeting (Saratoga Elementary School SACC 2 Room) - All homeowners are welcome to come and observe our Board Meeting.

3.  Our next Community Yard Sale will be held on Saturday, September 17 with a rain date of Saturday, September 24. This is a great opportunity to sell those items around your house and garage you don't need anymore.
 
4.  October 22 (Saturday - Rain Date is Saturday, October 29)  Community Fest (soccer field)  1 - 5 pm - Bring a side dish to share, there will be family games and plenty of activities for the kids. The TGCA Board will provide hamburgers and hot dogs. Please join us for our annual gathering.
Children's Costume Contest 3 pm!

Welcome to the Terra Grande Community Association in Springfield, Virginia


The Community Association is overseen by the resident elected board. The goal of the board is to foster homeowners' pride in the neighborhood and encourage a sense of community. In doing so, we hope to increase our property values. Each member of the board is committed to making the Terra Grande Community the best subdivision in which to live.

Our annual meeting is coming up on Tuesday, September 27, 2016 at Saratoga Elementary School. The meeting will be starting at 7 PM. We encourage you to join us, especially for this meeting as the Board will be presenting plans for the Tennis Court and the Basketball Court located below the Soccer Field. The Board is committed to taking action on these two areas. We will also be conducting a vote on a special assessment for the purpose of funding the plans we present.


Also during our meeting, we will be providing updates on other projects and issues effecting our community and we will be looking for volunteers who are willing to help on the Board and within the Community.
